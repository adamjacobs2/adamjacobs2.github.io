About Me

I'm Adam Jacobs, an electrical and computer engineering graduate student at the University of Florida. My background spans computer architecture, digital systems, and software development, with a focus on writing efficient, reliable code for constrained hardware environments in the aerospace domain.

After completing my BS in Computer Engineering at UF, I'm continuing my graduate studies while preparing to join Honeywell Aerospace as an Embedded Software Engineer. My long-term goal is to contribute software to a Martian mission, building the kind of systems that have to work right the first time.
